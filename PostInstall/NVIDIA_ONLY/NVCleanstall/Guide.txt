1. Run NvCleanstall.
2. Follow Steps 1-3; in Step 2, consider ticking Physx too.
   - Installs only the Graphic driver and Control panel.
3. Click the top option when prompted to install.
4. In the Nvidia Installer, follow the instructions without making any changes.
   - DO NOT close NvCleanstall.
5. After the installation is complete, close NvCleanstall and restart.
6. Done. Your driver is now installed.
